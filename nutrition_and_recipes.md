# Nutrition & Recipe Guide: The Liver-Heart Hybrid Diet

This guide combines the **Mediterranean Diet** (proven for fatty liver) and the **DASH Diet** (standard for heart failure).

## Golden Rules
1. **Low Sodium:** No added salt at the table. Use herbs, lemon, and spices for flavor.
2. **High Fiber:** 25-35g per day to help clear liver fat.
3. **Vitamin D Boost:** Include fatty fish (salmon/mackerel) at least 2x per week.
4. **No Liquid Sugar:** Replace soda/juice with water or hibiscus tea.

## Top Foods for You
*   **The Liver Protectors:** Artichokes, coffee (black), leafy greens, walnuts.
*   **The Heart Helpers:** Oats, berries, beans, garlic.
*   **Vitamin D Sources:** Salmon, sardines, egg yolks (limited to 1/day), fortified soy milk.

## Quick Recipes

### 1. Heart-Healthy Baked Salmon (Vitamin D Rich)
*   **Ingredients:** 150g Salmon fillet, 1 tsp olive oil, lemon slices, dried oregano, black pepper.
*   **Method:** Place salmon on foil. Drizzle oil, herbs, and lemon. Bake at 200°C for 12-15 mins.
*   **Why:** High in Omega-3s for the liver and Vitamin D for your deficiency.

### 2. Liver-Cleansing "Zoodles" & Turkey
*   **Ingredients:** 1 large zucchini (spiraled), 100g ground turkey, 2 tomatoes (crushed), garlic, basil.
*   **Method:** Sauté garlic and turkey. Add tomatoes and basil. Simmer. Toss in zucchini noodles for 2 mins.
*   **Why:** Low carb and very low sodium.
