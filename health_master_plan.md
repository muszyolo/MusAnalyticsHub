## Medical Strategy
### 1. Fatty Liver Management
*   **Goal:** Reduce liver fat and inflammation.
*   **Key Habit:** Eliminate all sugary beverages and ultra-processed snacks.
*   **Metric:** Monitor AST/ALT levels (as ordered by your doctor).

### 2. Heart Failure (Mild) Maintenance
*   **Goal:** Manage fluid balance and cardiac load.
*   **Key Habit:** Keep sodium below **2,000mg/day**.
*   **Red Flags:** Immediate call to doctor if you experience sudden weight gain (>1kg in 24hrs), increased shortness of breath, or swelling in ankles.

### 3. Vitamin D Recovery
*   **Goal:** Reach blood levels of 30-50 ng/mL.
*   **Habit:** Take prescribed supplement with a meal containing healthy fats (avocado/olive oil) for better absorption.

## Success Metrics
- [ ] **Daily:** Sodium check, Vitamin D supplement, 15 min movement.
- [ ] **Weekly:** Morning weight (consistent time/clothing).
- [ ] **Monthly:** Measurement of waist circumference.
