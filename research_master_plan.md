## 📚 Thesis Structure (The 7-Chapter Model)

### 1. Introduction
*   Problem statement: The burden on autism caregivers.
*   Research objectives and significance.

### 2. Literature Review
*   Systematic review of caregiver support frameworks.
*   The role of statistics and NLP in autism research.

### 3. Methodology
*   Cluster Analysis design.
*   Data collection protocols for the Taif setting.

### 4. Cluster Analysis Results
*   Identifying caregiver profiles and segmentation.
*   Statistical validation.

### 5. Targeted Counselling Framework
*   Developing interventions based on cluster profiles.

### 6. NLP-Driven Applications
*   Implementation of NLP for qualitative data synthesis or tool development.

### 7. Conclusion & Recommendations
*   Synthesis of findings and future research directions.

## 🛠️ Daily Research Habits
Consistency is key to a 1-year journey.

*   **Writing:** Minimum 500 words per day (Draft or notes).
*   **Reading:** 2 high-impact papers per week.
*   **Data:** 30 minutes of data cleaning or exploration.
*   **Organization:** Weekly audit of citations (Zotero/Mendeley).
