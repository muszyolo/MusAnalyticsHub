# Exercise Routine: Safe Movement for Heart & Liver

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Safety First:** If you feel dizzy, have chest pain, or sudden extreme shortness of breath, **STOP** immediately and rest. Contact your doctor if symptoms persist.

## The "Talk Test"
While exercising, you should be able to hold a conversation but not sing. This is the "Sweet Spot" for mild heart failure—effective but safe.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)
**Goal:** Consistency over Intensity.
*   **Activity:** Flat-surface walking.
*   **Frequency:** 5 days a week.
*   **Duration:** Start with 10 minutes. Add 2 minutes every 3 days until you reach 30 minutes.
*   **Intensity:** 3/10 on the effort scale.

## Phase 2: Building Capacity (Weeks 5-8)
**Goal:** Increase metabolic rate.
*   **Activity:** Brisk walking or water aerobics.
*   **Frequency:** 4-5 days a week.
*   **Duration:** 30 minutes.
*   **Strength:** Add 2 days of very light resistance bands (Seated rows, Wall push-ups).
*   **Intensity:** 4/10 on the effort scale.

## Daily Stretching (Liver Health)
*   **Lateral Stretch:** Reach one arm over your head and lean to the side (gentle rib cage expansion).
*   **Cat-Cow:** On all fours, gently arch and round your back to improve core circulation.
